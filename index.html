Who am I?

I am a Human-Computer Interaction Design student (specialization in Intelligent Systems) currently enrolled in the EIT Digital Master School, where I had the chance to study in two different countries and universities, namely Aalto University in Finland and University of Twente in the Netherlands.

Researching and designing the interaction that happens between user and technology has been my passion for several years. My Bachelor's studies gave me a solid basis in cognitive sciences and user-interface design, which combined with my previous education in computer science allows me to both design and implement my ideas.

On top of this, the minor in Entrepreneurship and Innovation of my Master's has given me the mindset and tools to validate and execute a business idea, which makes me really comfortable in working in a hectic and unpredictable environment of a startup.

I am currently looking for an internship to work on my Master's thesis.If you have an interesting opportunity to propose, or you are interested in more details about my background, please check my CV and do not hesitate to contact me!

Experiences & Projects

Beathoven

With Beathoven, we researched new ways to allow deaf people to experience music. To achieve this goal, we tried to combine different sensory modalities such as haptics and sight. We therefore implemented a vibrating forearm glove, augmenting the experience with VR.

I contributed by implementing the connection to Spotify APIs, and coding some parts using Processing and Unity.

Jammin'

The idea behind the project was to create a platform that allows musicians to find others to play with, based on their musical taste and commitment.

I have implemented the prototype using ReactJS + Firebase as back-end.

Check project website

Roomiego

For the Startup Experience course at Aalto University, we have been working on Roomiego - an online platform that allows one to find a perfect match to share his future flat with.

The whole team went through the several stages of creating the company, such as defining our values, the shareholders' agreement, user research, marketing plan and others.

Check project website

JustaCar

For the Design project course in Aalto University, we were asked to design new services regarding mobility-as-a-services (MAAS). After our team went through the divergent phase of design and explored several opportunities, we eventually focused on Justacar.
Justacar is a distributed car rental service that offers only low-end vehicles (the insight of focusing on these vehicles came from the user research). We then created and tested a prototype, making sure that the business would also be profitable.
